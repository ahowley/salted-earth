## Overview

Something of a weed, the extremely thin, near-transparent roots that are often
visible above surface sand after winds shift the
[sandlevel](/reference/reference.md#sandlevel) and
[amplishear](/reference/reference.md#amplishear) give this weed-like, small,
light, grassy plant the appearance of floating.

## Lifespan

Weeks/months

## Consumed by

- [People](/people/people.md)
- Most plant-eating creatures

## Notes

Hoverblade (or hoverblades, interchangeable in most circumstances, prevelance
varies by region) is one of the only elements of Ulduran flora which seem to
sprout year-round, in any climate regularly traversed on the planet.

It grows and dies and spreads quickly and shallow, although like with most
plants here, the roots extend much deeper than the blades themselves. Due to
these properties, the almost-exclusively nomadic people of Uldura perform a sort
of collaborative husbandry for these plants, one of the only forms of
agriculture I've witnessed. The seeds line the roots like we might picture
spider eggs lining a web, making them somewhat easy to scrape and spread by
hand, often increasing yield over natural spread, since seeds typically grow
themselves underground, but don't seem to sproud until separated by being
uncovered during shifting winds.

This has created some routes which several groups, otherwise unaffiliated with
one another, will follow in something of a rhythm, albeit over a somewhat large
time span. Some arrage this, but most stumble into this pattern by having landed
on similar survival instincts and desert familiarity.

Basically - floating desert grass.
