## Overview

A bizarre, tongue-like, leaf-ish thing that nonetheless seems to be flora, if
only due to its behaviors (or lack thereof).

## Lifespan

2-3 years

## Consumed by

- [People](/people/people.md)

## Notes

These things look _bizarre_, like the desert has a 1-2 foot long tongue sticking
out at random locations. Apparently, they get much larger, but the common and
widespread ones are closer to that size.

They are a faded yellow color, something like sun-bleached corn might look like
here, but truly look like an exposed, fleshy muscle otherwise. Nonetheless, they
appear to be made of some sort of fibrous material, not meat, and are often used
for cordage and rope. Skilled tailors (or equivalent) are even able to fashion
it into a low-cost clothing option as emergency replacements, etc.

They are edible, and prepared into all sorts of different salads, stews, and
even teas, on special occasion. Forgive me, I've never tried them.
