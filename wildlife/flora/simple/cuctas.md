## Overview

A spiny, tree-like structure, varying wildly in size, venom content, and
drinkable water.

## Lifespan

Unknown, but they appear like the great redwoods from back home, so one could
hypothesize they could live for many centuries.

## Consumed By

- [Skampfoot](/wildlife/fauna/peaceful/skampfoot.md)

## Notes

There are many, many, many types of Cuctas, ranging from small stick-like shrubs
to strange rock-like things to interconnected, indistinguishable webs sprawling
out like a very pointy forest. Some are so high you might think them landforms.

Their needles contain vital nutrients for most plant-eating creatures in the
desert, and they store water in a somewhat-refridgerated internal hollow
compartment powered by absorbing the remains of
[Icepetals](/wildlife/fauna/peaceful/icepetal.md) that have fallen in the
vicinity.

Nothing is common here, but these are certainly some of the most common plants
you will encounter. Fortunately, too, as they will keep you alive.
