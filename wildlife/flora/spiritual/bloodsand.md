## Notes

This strange, light-pink sand swirls across the dunes in rare patches which,
despite their light, pastel hue, appear vibrant amidst the unmoving sandy
monoliths around them.

It is unknown what creates this sand - is it a larger mineral broken down and
distributed, or a proper organism? All that is known is that it acts as thinking
quicksand, coordinating grains of salt and earth in a symphonic way.

Local cultures tell us that bloodsand can be used as a way for
[the Elders](/elders/elders.md) to judge our alignment and movement with the
Primordia. Despite my skepticism, it is true that it becomes much more dangers
when those traversing it carry with them violent intent.
