## Overview

These strange, entropic beings are some sort of lithoteric life, often generally
triangular, appearing as floating rocks. Extremely dangerous.

## Diet

Unknown

## Lifespan

Unknown

## Notes

It is not clear if these dangerous, floating stones are a form of life, per se,
nor is almost anything known about them, as encountering one in any circumstance
will either lead to a miracle escape at a dead sprint, or death and
dematerialization.

They are made of either stone or metal, although it is unclear which and how,
and through some undocumented means, are known for removing all trace of
sentient creatures who come across them from the Primordia.

They are capable of floating by some means, and produce a low hum in a small
radius around them that seems to levitate dust and sand at a very consistent (or
perhaps resonant?) distance from their "body". This creates a sort of
sand-shield that, when sufficiently agitated or travelling, can make it nearly
impossible to see the being itself.

Don't be curious.
