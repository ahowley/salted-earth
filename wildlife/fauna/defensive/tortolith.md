## Notes

These hulking creatures are lithoteric, meaning their bodies are made almost
entirely from minerals, with only a few thin pieces of tissue and, presumably, a
heart and mind to keep the pieces together.

These are four-legged beasts with a large stone shell and a single, piercing
horn that can be longer than their head. They spend almost their entire lives,
from the time they are a calf the size of a pebble, eating bleachgrass, needing
every possible scrap of minerals and nutrients they provide to continue to
survive and grow.

As such, they are also fiercly territorial, and will absolutely try to prevent
anything else alive from staying that way long if they wander into their grass.
They appear to have no eyes, sensing their location via other means.
