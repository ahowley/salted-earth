## Overview

Stingcrawlers resemble something like a centipede on our world, only they have
two conjoined bodies. They are moderately venomous, although rarely deadly.

## Diet

- [Cuctas needles](/wildlife/flora/simple/cuctas.md)
- Non-lithoteric creature blood, when desperate

## Lifespan

- 1 year

## Notes

These small but irritating pests are not always aggressive, but most of the
time, they should be treated as such - when people travel the surface, they
almost always harvest nearly every Cuctas they can, to the maximum extent
possible without killing it. As the only food source for a Stingcrawler, this
almost always leaves them hungry, leading them to search for their unfortunate
secondary food source, blood.

They don't hunt, nor attempt to maintain a parasitic relationship, instead
simply biting with a localized, flesh-dissolving venom, creating a wound from
which to absorb what they can, as fast as they can. Often, in a sleeping victim,
by the time the bite is painful enough to wake them up, they are seen already
scurrying away.

This has earned them another nickname - "Sucksleep." It is not uncommon to wish
someone a good night by saying the playful, but somewhat horrifying phrase,
"Don't let the Sucksleeps bite!"
