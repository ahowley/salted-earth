## Overview

Difficult to categorize - look like a blueish-white bat made of moisture, with
feathers made of icy flower petals

## Diet

Unknown, but presumably some kind of ice, based on when they appear and their
appearance

## Lifespan

A few hours, at best.

## Notes

One of the more exotic creatures to still be calm and peaceful when encountered.
These snowflake-like beings come out on most cold nights commonly enough you're
bound to encounter them if you spend any serious time here. They could likely be
consumed for hydration as-is, although the locals take protecting them very
seriously, for spiritual reasons.

When they die, or evaporate, they leave behind feather-light flakes of frozen
vapor that had formed into their wings' feathers, leaving a tiny smattering of
ice on top of the sand like frozen dew in broken patches each morning.
