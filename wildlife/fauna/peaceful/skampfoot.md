## Overview

A small, four-legged rodent with a small, pointed snout and paws that look like
shovels.

## Diet

- [Bloodsand](/wildlife/flora/spiritual/bloodsand.md)
- [Cuctas](/wildlife/flora/simple/cuctas.md) Needles

## Lifespan

Unknown, but if it is indeed something akin to a rodent, I would estimate 4-10
years.

## Notes

These adorable little creatures are calm as they come, and seem intelligent in a
way far beyond what I am used to seeing in a rodent.

The locals tell me that Skampfoots commune with the desert and primarily eat
[Bloodsand](/wildlife/flora/spiritual/bloodsand.md), a substance I didn't
actually know was edible at all. But it is believed that the energy stored in
the sand not only feeds them, but may even be the source of new baby Skamps.

They have camoflauged, sandy fur, white with a tinge of gray-blue, matching the
sprawl of Uldura. The only exception is their navy-blue nose, which you'll
sometimes find poking out of the dunes when they're burrowing for warmth at
night.

They are quirky things, eager to interact and with a lot of wisdom to offer, for
those willing to listen.
