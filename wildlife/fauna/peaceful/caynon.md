Looks like... a dog. With little wings.

## Lifespan

9-17 years, depending on the breed.

## Notes

It is _unfathomable_ to me that these exist here, having evolved, presumably,
independently, but alas, there is much that I don't know about our universe.

There are many breeds of caynon, but aside from the fact they are not
technically a domesticated breed, they are nonetheless common pets, hunting
companions, and guards for travelling peoples on Uldura.

They look... truly like what I would call a dog, but with little wings that
almost always barely work, and are very cute.
