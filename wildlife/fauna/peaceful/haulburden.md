## Overview

You'll think I'm making up the name, but I swear this is a good-faith
translation. They haul burdens.

## Diet

Most edible plants

## Lifespan

15-23 years

## Notes

You'd be forgiven for thinking these beasts look a small bit like a cow or bull
appears to us, if checking from the front - in reality, this lightweight, very
muscular beast of burden (say nothing.) has a frill around its neck (a bit like
a mane made from scales). Indeed its whole body is made of scales, although it
is most likely still warm-blooded (I have not seen a cold-blooded creature on
Uldura).

They wander with caravans nonstop, hauling people (especially injured) and goods
across vast dunes and open plains of
[hoverblade](/wildlife/flora/simple/hoverblade.md). They aren't typically used
as riding animals, although they could be trained for this if you have the
proper equipment (bareback is quick difficult due to the frill/mane).

Additionally, people here can't [atone](/reference/reference.md#atonement) when
their feet aren't in contact with the sand, or at least, it seems more difficult
for them - whatever exactly "it" may be. This means riding animals in general,
and a pursuit of flight magic or technology, is generally extremely rare.
