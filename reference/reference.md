## Amplishear

Seems to refer to the "complexity" or general profile of a given portion of
desert. In other words, a measure of scale, dimensionality, and shapeliness all
in one. _"The Dune Sea has the greatest amplishear on Uldura."_

## Atonement

My best efforts at a translation, this is the name of the process by which
someone born on Uldura may hear the vibrations of the world in order to sense
the presence of what they cannot see beneath the sand.

## Ferroteric

Subset of [Lithoteric](#lithoteric), a living thing made at least in part of
metal (not a shell or armor, but a part of the mechanics of life).

## Ferrovoric

Subset of [Lithovoric](#lithovoric), a living thing which consumes metal to
sustain life.

## Lithoteric

A living thing made at least in part of stone (not a shell or armor, but a part
of the mechanics of life).

## Lithovoric

A living thing which consumes minerals/stone to sustain life.

## Sandlevel

A term often used in place of how we might use altitude.

## Settlesand

A term often used in place of how we might use "sea level," relative to the
level of the sand
[the Harmonic Blossom](/world/regions/the-harmonic-blossom/the-harmonic-blossom.md)'s
banks.
