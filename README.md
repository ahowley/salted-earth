# Collected Field Notes on the World of Uldura

Their world is lifeless and blistering, a blue-white sun scalding the surface
and everything on it during the long, long days, and sweat that dries to ice off
your skin during the longer nights. White sand sprawls, as though this place
were turned to ash of the palest blue, and as though its remains were spread in
a garden that didn't have the room to hold it.

Below you will find all of the information I have collected and documented
during my time here.
