## Description

Lesia is said to be a gash hewn into Uldura by some unknown chaos. She bleeds a
river of blood whose winding path changes often, and hasn't been seen in many,
many years.

Her blood speaks on the wind, whispering echoes of death and chaos which can be
heard long before she is seen or felt.

When Lesia flows, all life flees. This is one of many reasons no one stands
still in Uldura for long.
