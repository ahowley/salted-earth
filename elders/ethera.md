## Description

Ethera is not a soul themself, but rather a shared manifestation of the souls of
Uldura. Some cultures depict them as a ferryperson, chartering souls to their
rest; others depict them as a great maw, hungry to consume us all.

Although no living person seems to have ever witnessed Ethera directly, it is
widely accepted that they have a physical appearance not dissimilar from most of
the rest of us, but shrouded in fog and impossible to bring into focus. An
unsettling blur of what was.
