## Description

Vela is an ascendant being who once was believed to take the form of a grand
dragon hewn of crystallized air. He has long since vanished, presumably having
become indistinguishable from the wind surrounding him.

Believed to be the most tempermental of the Elders (aside from
[Lesia](./lesia.md)), Vela is often worshipped loudly and openly, in hopes of
tempering the sandstorms and bringing milder weather.

Mild weather, of course, never comes; still, the people here keep faith.
