## Description

Chama is said to have been born among mortals, but lacked a solid form. She
existed only in the sacred pond in her settlement, taking form during full moons
as a resonant shell of pure water. The shape of this creature varies widely by
depiction, and it is unclear whether this is because the shape changes or
because she has been encountered so rarely.

She is openly praised as a lifebringer, and is believed to battle with Vela to
condense water such that it may fall.
