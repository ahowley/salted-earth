## Description

Insa is, in some ways, the most grounded of the Elders. Living deep beneath the
sand, and seen only during rare religious ceremonies and cosmic events, Insa
appears as a grand wyrm made of glass, with wings that form from vibrating sand
when she surfaces.

I say she - this is how the locals refer to her. She is universally revered, and
feared; she blesses and protects those aligned with her, and those who intend
harm to her faithful quickly disappear beneath the sands.

Or so they say. I have never seen her. I have, however, heard her humming roar,
deep beneath the pale ocean.
