## Description

Fara is the name for the eternal, golden-cyan flame burning relentlessly in the
Valley of Concordance. Believed to be a force of endless destruction on one
hand, but the flame of life on the other, it is often believed that Fara singed
the Valley into existence, and something about their presence there upholds a
contract that allows life on Uldura to persist.

> _"Why does the flame never cease?"_
