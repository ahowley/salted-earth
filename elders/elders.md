# The Elders

The Elders are a rare breed of being found across our universe; a creature great
or small who was, for one reason or another, born devoid of mortality or
otherwise granted immortality.

It is and has always been unclear what their relationship with the Primordia
is - do they command it, serve it, or are they merely passengers alongside the
rest of us?

Elders on Uldura are more scarce than the place from whence I hail, but then, so
is everything else in this place. Still though, life seems to find a way.
Perhaps their faith is what allows them to see purpose in oceans and oceans of
porcelain dust.
