## Description

Cereba is a being the residents of Uldura will most likely tell you either isn't
real, or exists only by travelling between our minds. This fascinating force of
nature (or shared delusion, depending who and what you believe) has been known
to provide visions of the future, memories of the past, and a connection between
all thinking things.
