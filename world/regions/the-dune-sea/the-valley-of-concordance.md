# The Valley of Concordance

- [Home](/README.md)
- [World](../../world.md)
- [The Dune Sea](./the-dune-sea.md)

The Valley of Concordance sits deep in the heart of the
[Dune Sea](./the-dune-sea.md), and almost none have ever visited, including me.
This makes it hard to describe, although some folk tales exist depicting a
valley made of pure glass, a shell below which one can see through reality.

In the Northern portion of the Valley resides [Fara](/elders/fara.md), the
eternal flame.

What created such a place, and why, remains a mystery to me and everyone with
whom I have spoken.
