## Overview

Home to the vast majority of the [Water Golems](/people/golems.md) on Uldura,
Sharalum is a somewhat mythical place to most (including me). Impossible to
reach for a multitude of reasons, the main one being the Golems themselves,
almost nothing is known about this place, what happens to it during
(winter)[./the-harmonic-blossom.md#a-note-on-winter], or how the people here
survive.

All we know, we know from above - there is a color change in the water when
looking straight down at it that suggests some kind of reef-like construct.
Beyond that, your guess is a good as mine.
