# Uldura

- [Home](/README.md)

As I said before, this place is barren and lifeless in all but a few remote,
dedicated places, at least at first glance. There is no color here, except when
the spare [skampfoot](/wildlife/fauna/peaceful/skampfoot.md) pokes its nose out
into the cold.

Long days give way to longer nights; each is comprised of several of the days
I'm used to. The [elders](/elders/elders.md) here are primal and forceful,
making their presence known far more than we might be used to.

The people are resilient and strong, and fiercely communal, although infighting
does crop up from time to time. War, however, is unheard of. No one on this
world needs help to die.
